# Features

Discover the powerful tools and systems that make Lilia a leading roleplay framework.

---

<h3 style="margin-bottom5px; font-weight700;">Overview</h3>
<div style="margin-left20px; margin-bottom20px;">
  <p>Lilia is built with a focus on developer flexibility and player performance. It provides a robust suite of built-in systems that handle everything from character persistence to advanced UI interactions, allowing you to focus on creating unique roleplay experiences.</p>
</div>

---

<h3 style="margin-bottom5px; font-weight700;">Character System</h3>
<div style="margin-left20px; margin-bottom20px;">
  <details class="realm-shared no-icon">
    <summary>Multi-Character Support</summary>
    <div class="details-content">
      <ul>
        <li><strong>Multiple Characters Per Player</strong>Players can create and manage several different characters on the same server, each with their own unique identity, inventory, and progress</li>
        <li><strong>Main Character Selection</strong>Players can designate one character as their "main" for quick loading when joining the server</li>
        <li><strong>Character Switching</strong>Easily switch between characters without leaving the server through an intuitive menu</li>
        <li><strong>Character Limit Management</strong>Server owners can set how many characters each player is allowed to create</li>
        <li><strong>Extra Character Slots</strong>Administrators can grant individual players bonus character slots beyond the standard limit</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
  </details>

  <details class="realm-shared no-icon">
    <summary>Character Information</summary>
    <div class="details-content">
      <ul>
        <li><strong>Custom Names</strong>Each character has their own unique name that other players will see</li>
        <li><strong>Physical Description</strong>Characters can have detailed descriptions that others can read when examining them</li>
        <li><strong>Model Selection</strong>Choose from available player models to represent your character's appearance</li>
        <li><strong>Faction Assignment</strong>Characters belong to specific factions (groups) that determine their role on the server</li>
        <li><strong>Class System</strong>Within factions, characters can have specialized classes with unique abilities or equipment</li>
        <li><strong>Character Attributes</strong>Track character stats like strength, endurance, or other custom attributes that affect gameplay</li>
        <li><strong>Character Flags</strong>Special permissions that unlock specific features, items, or abilities for individual characters</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
  </details>

  <details class="realm-shared no-icon">
    <summary>Character Recognition</summary>
    <div class="details-content">
      <ul>
        <li><strong>Name Recognition System</strong>Characters don't automatically know each other's names - they must introduce themselves first</li>
        <li><strong>Unknown Names Display</strong>Unrecognized characters appear as generic descriptions (like "Unknown Man" or "Unknown Woman") until properly introduced</li>
        <li><strong>Recognition Management</strong>Players can introduce themselves to others, building relationships naturally through roleplay</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
  </details>

  <details class="realm-shared no-icon">
    <summary>Character Persistence</summary>
    <div class="details-content">
      <ul>
        <li><strong>Automatic Saving</strong>All character data, including inventory, position, and stats, saves automatically</li>
        <li><strong>Cross-Session Continuity</strong>Characters remember everything between play sessions</li>
        <li><strong>Character History</strong>Track each character's actions, warnings, and administrative notes</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
  </details>
</div>

<h3 style="margin-bottom5px; font-weight700;">Inventory & Items</h3>
<div style="margin-left20px; margin-bottom20px;">
  <details class="realm-shared no-icon">
    <summary>Grid-Based Inventory</summary>
    <div class="details-content">
      <ul>
        <li><strong>Visual Grid System</strong>Inventory uses an intuitive grid layout where items take up physical space based on their size</li>
        <li><strong>Drag and Drop</strong>Move items around by clicking and dragging them to different slots</li>
        <li><strong>Item Rotation</strong>Rotate items to fit them more efficiently in your inventory space</li>
        <li><strong>Weight System</strong>Items have weight, and carrying too much will slow you down or prevent you from picking up more</li>
        <li><strong>Inventory Capacity</strong>Each character has a limited amount of space, encouraging strategic item management</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
  </details>

  <details class="realm-shared no-icon">
    <summary>Container System</summary>
    <div class="details-content">
      <ul>
        <li><strong>Bags and Containers</strong>Special items like backpacks, suitcases, or bags that provide additional storage space</li>
        <li><strong>Nested Inventories</strong>Open containers to access their own separate inventory grid</li>
        <li><strong>Container Variety</strong>Different containers offer different amounts of space and weight capacity</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
  </details>

  <details class="realm-shared no-icon">
    <summary>Item Interactions</summary>
    <div class="details-content">
      <ul>
        <li><strong>Use Items</strong>Click items to use them - eat food, drink beverages, equip weapons, etc.</li>
        <li><strong>Drop Items</strong>Remove items from your inventory and drop them on the ground for others to find</li>
        <li><strong>Give Items</strong>Transfer items directly to other players through a trade interface</li>
        <li><strong>Examine Items</strong>View detailed information about any item, including descriptions and properties</li>
        <li><strong>Item Cooldowns</strong>Some items have cooldown periods to prevent spam or abuse</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
  </details>

  <details class="realm-shared no-icon">
    <summary>Storage Entities</summary>
    <div class="details-content">
      <ul>
        <li><strong>Persistent Storage</strong>Place storage containers in the world that save their contents even when no one is nearby</li>
        <li><strong>Password Protection</strong>Lock storage containers with passwords to prevent unauthorized access</li>
        <li><strong>Shared Storage</strong>Multiple players can access the same storage if they know the password</li>
        <li><strong>Storage Management</strong>Administrators can create, remove, and manage storage containers</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
  </details>
</div>

<h3 style="margin-bottom5px; font-weight700;">Chat & Communication</h3>
<div style="margin-left20px; margin-bottom20px;">
  <details class="realm-shared no-icon">
    <summary>Chat Channels</summary>
    <div class="details-content">
      <ul>
        <li><strong>Local Chat (IC)</strong>Speak in-character to players near you - others far away won't see your messages</li>
        <li><strong>Out-of-Character Chat (OOC)</strong>Talk to all players on the server about non-roleplay topics</li>
        <li><strong>Whisper</strong>Send quiet messages that only very close players can see</li>
        <li><strong>Yell</strong>Shout messages that can be heard from further away than normal speech</li>
        <li><strong>Admin Chat</strong>Private channel for staff members to communicate without players seeing</li>
        <li><strong>Event Chat</strong>Special channel for event coordinators to make server-wide announcements</li>
        <li><strong>Local Event Chat</strong>Event announcements that only affect players in a specific area</li>
        <li><strong>Help Chat</strong>Request assistance from staff members when you need support</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
  </details>

  <details class="realm-shared no-icon">
    <summary>Chat Features</summary>
    <div class="details-content">
      <ul>
        <li><strong>Rich Text Formatting</strong>Messages can include colors, formatting, and special styling</li>
        <li><strong>Command Autocomplete</strong>Start typing a command and the chatbox suggests completions</li>
        <li><strong>Chat History</strong>Scroll back through previous messages to review conversations</li>
        <li><strong>OOC Cooldown</strong>Prevents spam by limiting how often players can use out-of-character chat</li>
        <li><strong>OOC Toggle</strong>Administrators can enable or disable OOC chat server-wide</li>
        <li><strong>Chat Radius</strong>Configure how far different types of messages can be heard</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
  </details>
</div>

<h3 style="margin-bottom5px; font-weight700;">Protection & Anti-Cheat</h3>
<div style="margin-left20px; margin-bottom20px;">
  <details class="realm-shared no-icon">
    <summary>Anti-Exploit Systems</summary>
    <div class="details-content">
      <ul>
        <li><strong>Cheat Detection</strong>Automatically detects suspicious player behavior that might indicate cheating</li>
        <li><strong>Alt Account Detection</strong>Identifies when players are using multiple accounts (alts) on the same server</li>
        <li><strong>Exploit Notifications</strong>Staff members receive alerts when potential cheating is detected</li>
        <li><strong>Automatic Logging</strong>All suspicious activity is recorded for administrator review</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
  </details>

  <details class="realm-shared no-icon">
    <summary>Player Protection</summary>
    <div class="details-content">
      <ul>
        <li><strong>Spawn Protection</strong>New players or those who just respawned are temporarily protected from damage</li>
        <li><strong>Anti-Prop Kill</strong>Prevents players from using physics props to kill others unfairly</li>
        <li><strong>Anti-Prop Surf</strong>Stops players from exploiting physics to fly or move unrealistically</li>
        <li><strong>World Entity Protection</strong>Prevents players from damaging or removing map entities without permission</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
  </details>
